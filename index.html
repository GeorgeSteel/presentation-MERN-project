RAEE

Reciclado De Aparatos Eléctricos Y Electrónicos It is a company that collects electronic waste, such as laptops, TV’s, power supplies, etc. Hardware in general.

Vision and Mission of the company

Mission: As a company, our purpose is to improve the process of eliminating electronic waste to reduce the environment impact, which in turn seek to promote jobs and raise awareness about the importance of recycling.

Vision: RAEE Aguascalientes seeks to be one of the main companies that collect and recycle electronic waste with certifications and recognition in the state of Aguascalientes and the region.

Objective

Problem:

Currently the company RAEE has no way to carry out the control of the trucks that enter the company, so they are susceptible to theft.

Solution:

Develop a web application that has a user management and a truck management where you can query even the routes of these with Google Maps API.

Hypothesis

The Company gets control over his merchandise avoiding thefts, minimizing losses that the company would have if they doesn’t have any control of his trucks.
